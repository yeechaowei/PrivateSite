Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

In short: Guilt Eraser is a fully local counter app. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

1. What Data We Collect

None. Guilt Eraser does not collect, track, or share any personal data. All your tap counts, accumulated values, labels, selected devil images, sound preferences, language settings, and user profiles are stored exclusively on your device using local Hive database files. No data is sent to any server, analytics service, or third party.

2. Custom Images

Guilt Eraser allows you to use images from your photo library as custom devil avatars. When you select an image, the app reads the image data from your photo library and stores a resized copy locally on your device. The original image is never uploaded or transmitted. You can delete custom images at any time from the Settings screen. The app requests the NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription permission solely for this purpose.

3. Audio Playback

Guilt Eraser plays built-in sound effects (WAV files) when you tap the devil. These sounds are bundled with the application and are played locally on your device. No audio data is recorded, transmitted, or shared.

4. Internet & Networking

Guilt Eraser is a fully offline app. It does not make any network connections. All devil images, sound effects, and app content are built into the application. No internet access is required at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

Guilt Eraser uses no third-party analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only external packages used are:

  • Hive — local database library (no network access)
  • image_picker — photo library access (local only)

None of these packages transmit data off your device.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

All data remains on your device for as long as the app is installed. When you uninstall Guilt Eraser, all related data (including tap counts, labels, custom images, and user profiles) is permanently deleted from your device. No online accounts or cloud backups are created within the app.

7. Multi-User Profiles

Guilt Eraser supports multiple local user profiles. Each profile's data (counts, labels, settings) is stored separately on your device. Profile switching is local-only and no data is shared between devices or accounts.

8. Children's Privacy

Guilt Eraser does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is designed for general audiences and contains no content that targets children specifically. All data remains entirely local with no online component.

9. App Store & Payment

Guilt Eraser is a paid download app (¥6 one-time purchase). The payment is processed entirely by Apple's App Store. The app itself contains no in-app purchases, subscriptions, advertisements, or additional payment mechanisms. The developer has no access to your payment information.

10. Policy Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
